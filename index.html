Why Choose Us?

Our IoT-based Smart Parking Platform provides a world-class unique set of technology and data capabilities to solve for parking management and congestion in our cities. -

Real Time Monitoring

Sensing real time occupancy and sharing the information with the systems of a business via Visualization dashboards or with a customer (for online booking purposes).

Vision Computing

Leverages AI and ML in the form of computer vision to detect via CCTV feeds regarding availability of parking esp. open-street parking.

Predictive Model

Patent-pending machine learning based algorithm looks at both historic datasets and real-world events to predict future occupancy in order for business to manage pricing and/or demand.

Data Analytics

Data layer integrates seamlessly with existing sensors, parking meters, legacy systems for tracking both current and past data of parking on a single interface.
