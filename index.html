Why Choose Us?

We are providing a Smart Parking Platform with a unique set of technology and data capabilities to solve parking management and congestion in our cities. Our solution will fit for any office, residences, commercial places and stand alone parking lots.

Admin Panel:

We can replace the manual parking booking and management and digitize the same for ease of use. Using our admin panel any facility can get complete control along with a report for the parking management system.

Digital Payment:

EazyPark can handle end to end payment processing for the parking solution (where ever needed). Easy to integrate payment system is available as a separate module for our application. Payment can be calculated based on in and out timing and collected via UPI, Cards, Wallets etc.

Space Management:

Managing the parking space, adding and modifying the space along with setting up the parking fees can be controlled by the parking facility owners. It provides a complete flexibility to the system. Any update will be instantly displayed via app.

Ticket Less Parking:

Manual ticketing system can be replaced via our backend admin app. This can be integrated with a complete payment solution and without any payment solution. Check in and check out of cars can be managed using our app by the parking gate keeper. Complete reporting will be provided to management.

Value Added Services:

Our solution can also provide different value added services, whatever your car would require. For example, car insurance, washing, repairing and many more services will be provided via the EazyPark team. We will be the one stop shop for your cars.

Merchandise:

Any car related parts and merchandises will be available for sale using EazyPark app and these will be applicable at a competitive pricing.

Refund Policy
Please find below the details for refund policy.

1. Customer will receive a full/partial refund if they cancel any parking before parking time starts
2. Refund will be processed to the same mode of payment which was used during the parking booking
3. No refund will be made if customer did not turn up (no show) after booking a parking spot and customer will be charged for first hour of booking in such cases.
4. Management holds the right to cancel any confirmed booking in case of any unavoidable circumstances occur at the parking space. Customer will receive a full refund for such cases.
5. Service charges will be applicable if customer cancels the booking at any given time. Refund amount will be = total amount - service charge.

Refund Amount:

Refund fees vary by city and by facility. Parking management has every right to decide how much percentage of refund is payable to customer.
Cancelling a Booked Parking
You can cancel parking at any time, before the booking time starts.
If you cancel after the start time, you will be charged a cancellation fee.

To cancel before parking start time:

1. Customer can cancel booked parking only via our mobile application
2. Open our mobile app & click on “My Booking” section
3. Choose the parking, which you want to cancel, from “Upcoming Booking”
4. Click on the ellipsis icon (three dots) and choose cancel booking
5. Customer will receive a cancellation confirmation

Other Terms:

1. Cancellation is only allowed before your parking time starts
2. Customer will be charged for first hour if you do not cancel booking and do not turn up as well
3. Management has rights to auto cancel booking if customer did not turn up within first 30 minutes from booking time and customer will also be charged for first hour.
4. Management reserved the right to cancel and confirmed booking in case of any unwanted circumstances occur in the premises.

CANCELLATION FEES

Cancellation fees vary by city and by product, but are typically between Rs 20 - Rs 50.
